Request Account Deletion

This page explains how users of Your App Name can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your account, please email us at:

your@email.com

Use the subject line: Delete My Account
Include the phone number used to sign in to the app.

Data that will be deleted

  • User account information
  • Phone number used for authentication
  • Any app-related usage data linked to the account

Data retention

After a deletion request, data is permanently deleted within 30 days, unless retention is required by law.
